Everything is logged. See Privacy Statement. Type 'menu' at any time for options. You may need to refresh your browser to wake Chappie up.

Why?

  • Help veterans and their families get answers faster.
  • Cut costly calls and correspondence.

How?

Chappie's conversation flow is modelled on a legal client interview. Chappie's knowledge base contains the full text of over 250 factsheets from DVA's website. Chappie attempts to identify the most relevant factsheet by progressively narrowing scope. Then Chappie answers questions from the content of the most relevant factsheet.

Chappie exploits:

  • Commercial Cloud-based Artifical Intelligence API's for Natural Language Processing and Search.
  • Heuristics defined by Gov Law Tech in collaboration with DVA.

Special?

Compared to other similar Government 'FAQ Bots', Chappie:

  • Does not require the Department to maintain and curate a separate knowledge base. Chappie reads published factsheets directly.
  • Is completely open source.
